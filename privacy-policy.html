Privacy Policy for Bookmark Kanban

Last updated: [2025.03.26]

Data Collection

Bookmark Kanban does not collect or transmit any personal data. The extension only:

  • Accesses your browser bookmarks to display them
  • Stores your layout preferences locally in your browser
  • Loads favicon images from websites you've bookmarked

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

The extension uses Google's favicon service to display website icons. No personal information is sent with these requests.
